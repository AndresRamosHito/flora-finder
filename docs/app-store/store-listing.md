## Subtitle / short tagline

## Short description

Document wild orchids, support responsible identification, and help protect sensitive species locations.

## Long description

OrquIDea is a citizen-science platform for documenting wild orchids and supporting conservation-focused observation.

The app helps users explore orchid observations, learn about species, contribute field records, and participate in a conservation-minded community. OrquIDea is built around a strict observe-only principle: wild orchids should be photographed and documented, not collected or disturbed.

Sensitive species locations are handled carefully. Public displays may generalize or hide precise locality data to reduce the risk of poaching, habitat damage, or illegal trade.

Key features:

- Explore community orchid observations
- View orchid records on a map
- Browse orchid species information
- Submit new sightings with photos and field notes
- Report suspected illegal orchid trade
- Connect with orchid societies and conservation groups
- Use a mobile-first interface designed for field conditions

OrquIDea is operated by OrchidArc and focuses on education, conservation, and responsible citizen science.

## Spanish description

OrquIDea es una plataforma de ciencia ciudadana para documentar orquídeas silvestres y apoyar la conservación.

La app permite explorar avistamientos, aprender sobre especies, contribuir registros de campo y participar en una comunidad enfocada en la observación responsable. OrquIDea sigue una regla central: observar y fotografiar, nunca colectar ni perturbar orquídeas silvestres.

Las ubicaciones de especies sensibles pueden ocultarse o generalizarse para reducir riesgos de saqueo, daño al hábitat o comercio ilegal.

Funciones principales:

- Explorar avistamientos comunitarios
- Ver registros en mapa
- Consultar información de especies
- Enviar observaciones con fotos y notas de campo
- Reportar posible comercio ilegal
- Conectar con sociedades y grupos de conservación
- Usar una interfaz móvil pensada para condiciones de campo
